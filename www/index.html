About Us

UFI Filters Group

UFI Filters is a global leader in filtration technology and thermal management.

Overview

SOFIMA is the brand marketed for the independent spare part network by the UFI Group Aftermarket Business Unit, which guarantees the same levels of quality, innovation and technology chosen by the OEM. The SOFIMA catalogue consists of over 2,800 references for cars, heavy duty, agricultural and motorbikes, to guarantee a coverage of over 96% of the European car fleet for each product family. There are 15 UFI aftermarket sales offices that guarantee a widespread presence in Europe, China, India, North America, North Africa and the Middle East.
In India Sofima is present in the LV (Light Vehicle) and HD (Heavy Duty) segment.
